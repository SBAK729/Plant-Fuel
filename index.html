FAQs

Find answers to your questions about our plant-based snacks and their health benefits.

What are the ingredients?

Our snacks are made from high-quality plant-based ingredients like lentils and quinoa. We prioritize natural flavorings and avoid additives or preservatives. This ensures you get the best nutrition without compromising on taste.

How do I store?

Store our snacks in a cool, dry place to maintain freshness. For optimal taste, consume them within the recommended date. Enjoy them as a quick snack or post-workout fuel!

What are the benefits?

Our snacks provide a high protein content, essential for muscle recovery and energy. They are also rich in fiber, promoting digestive health. Enjoy a guilt-free snack that fuels your active lifestyle!

Are they gluten-free?

Yes, all our products are gluten-free. We understand the importance of catering to various dietary needs. Enjoy our snacks worry-free!
